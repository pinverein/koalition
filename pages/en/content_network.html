Network

pin is looking forward to many collaborations!

Building a network, cooperating with organisations as well as individuals and consulting and involving people is one of pin's main priorities.
Therefore we want to create this platform in order to include and display contacts and information about cooperation partners as well as interested organisations. Thus visitors to the pin website can instantly gather information on numerous and multifaceted projects and offers within the political and socio-cultural landscape, especially on integrative projects and special offers for persons with experiences of displacement.
